About URLGent

A small utility to extract data from various resources on the Internet.

It's like Clearly or Instapaper but for all sorts of resources.

Privacy

How much of your data is captured?

Nothing really. URLGent doesn't set a cookie in your browser or track you in any other way. There is no session attached to you and your personally identifiable information (including IP, location, browser, etc) is not captured at any point.

What is stored?

  • a generalised event log (accessible on Stats page)
  • 60 minute* cache of all lookups made and their results (but not who made those requests)

* In early development the cleanup will be triggered manually so cache may remain in place for longer periods of time.
