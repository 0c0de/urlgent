About URLGent

A small utility to connect you directly to various videos and audio clips on the Internet.

It's like Clearly or Instapaper but for all sorts of resources.

The primary goal is privacy - you only connect to URLGent servers when looking up or viewing a video/audio resource.

It also removes bloat that the media sites generally put around the content. This translates to lower memory usage and longer battery life by scaling down on the resources necessary to render the content.

Privacy

How much of your data is captured?

Nothing really. URLGent doesn't set a cookie in your browser or track you in any other way. There is no session attached to you and your personally identifiable information (IP, location, browser used, etc) is not captured at any point.

In addition to that no files are ever physically stored on the server. Everything is simply streamed from the original location of the data.

What is stored?

  • a generalised event log (summarised numbers of page visits, requests made)
  • up to 7 day history of lookups made and their results (but not who made those requests)
