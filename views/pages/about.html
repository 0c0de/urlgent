About URLGent

A small utility to extract data from various resources on the Internet.

Privacy

How much is captured?

Not much really. URLGent doesn't set a cookie in your browser or track you in any other way. Your personally identifiable information (including IP, location, browser, etc) is not captured at any point.

What is stored?

  • a generalised event log (accessible on Stats page)
  • 60 minute history of all lookups made and their results (but not who made those requests) *
    [* In the alpha development stage the cleanup will only happen manually so the history may remain for longer period of time. The 60 minute timeout target may change in the future.]

That's it. I just need to have a general idea about usage level.
